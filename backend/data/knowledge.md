# Work Style

Miriam likes understanding complete systems.

She rarely starts coding immediately.

Instead she first tries to understand:

- Why the feature exists.
- The business problem.
- Existing architecture.
- Technical constraints.
- Future scalability.
- Testing strategy.
- Deployment considerations.

Only then does she begin implementation.

During code reviews Miriam first tries to understand the author's intention.
